# Cobalt Chapel Theme Brief

## Theme Name

Cobalt Chapel

## One-Sentence Concept

A sacred-tech blue theme that feels like a polished command center built inside an old stone chapel.

## Mood Keywords

- Cobalt
- Sacred-tech
- Polished stone
- Quiet authority
- Glass signal
- Premium utility
- Chapel shadow
- Controlled glow

## Ideal Use Cases

- SaaS dashboards and analytics tools
- AI tooling and model-operation interfaces
- Finance, governance, and audit products
- Developer tools and command surfaces
- Premium portfolio or agency microsites
- Presentation decks that need a serious but distinctive blue system

## Palette Direction

Use blue as the clear identity, but avoid default navy. Build around deep cobalt, chapel blue-black, oxidized teal, cloud off-white, graphite, glass blue, and a restrained warm gold accent.

Rough families:

- Deep cobalt and ultramarine for brand presence
- Blue-black and graphite for app surfaces
- Oxidized teal for secondary accents and active states
- Cloud white and pale stone for text and elevated surfaces
- Restrained gold for rare highlight, focus, or premium status
- Cool grey-blue for borders and muted text

## Accessibility and Contrast Notes

- Keep primary text high contrast against blue-black surfaces.
- Do not rely on cobalt versus teal alone for state differences; pair with value contrast.
- Gold should be used sparingly and tested on dark and light backgrounds.
- Provide at least one calm light surface token so the theme is not dark-only.

## Token Personality Notes

- Brand: deep cobalt, confident and clean, not corporate navy.
- Surface: stone blue-black and graphite with subtle cool depth.
- Accent: oxidized teal or glass blue for active controls and links.
- Danger: clear red leaning cool enough to fit the blue system.
- Success: teal-green, not generic traffic-light green.
- Warning: restrained gold or amber, used like candlelight rather than neon.
- Text: cloud white for primary text, blue-grey for muted text, charcoal for light surfaces.

## Suggested Preview and Listing Language

Cobalt Chapel is a polished blue Apocapalette theme pack for dashboards, AI tools, finance concepts, and premium utility interfaces. It combines sacred-tech cobalt, chapel-dark surfaces, glassy teal accents, and quiet off-white text into a complete tokenized color system.

## Avoid

- Do not drift into generic corporate navy.
- Do not make every token a blue variant; keep stone, cloud, teal, and gold distinct.
- Avoid bright gamer cyan as the main accent.
- Avoid low-contrast blue text on blue surfaces.
- Avoid making the theme feel ecclesiastical in a literal decorative way; the chapel cue should come from atmosphere, not icons.
