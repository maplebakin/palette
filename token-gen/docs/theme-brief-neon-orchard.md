# Neon Orchard Theme Brief

## Theme Name

Neon Orchard

## One-Sentence Concept

A fruit-bright cyber garden theme with tart green, digital pink, citrus orange, and dark leaf shadows.

## Mood Keywords

- Cyber fruit
- Acid green
- Social-first
- Playful commerce
- Arcade garden
- Bright signal
- Retro-future

## Ideal Use Cases

- Music apps and creator tools
- Ecommerce drops and retail landing pages
- Food, beverage, and pop-up brand concepts
- Social campaign graphics
- Arcade UI and playful game menus
- Event microsites
- High-energy prototype decks

## Palette Direction

Use controlled brights against grounded dark and cream surfaces. The palette should feel energetic but organized, with acid green, neon melon, hot pink, citrus orange, leaf black, cream white, and a small electric blue accent.

Rough families:

- Acid green and cyber lime for brand and primary action
- Neon melon and citrus orange for warm emphasis
- Hot pink for secondary accent and playful highlights
- Leaf black and deep green-black for dark surfaces
- Cream white for light surfaces and breathing room
- Electric blue for rare contrast pops
- Muted rind green for borders or subdued states

## Accessibility and Contrast Notes

- High-chroma colors need dark or cream pairings; avoid bright-on-bright text.
- Provide toned-down variants for hover, disabled, and subtle surface states.
- Warning and success can blur if both use green/yellow families; separate them clearly by hue and value.
- Do not let neon accents become the default text color.

## Token Personality Notes

- Brand: acid green or neon melon, confident and unmistakable.
- Surface: leaf black for dark mode, cream white for light product surfaces.
- Accent: hot pink, citrus orange, or electric blue depending on interaction priority.
- Danger: hot pink-red or punchy tomato, clearly separated from orange warning.
- Success: bright orchard green with a darker accessible pair.
- Warning: citrus orange or yellow-orange, not greenish.
- Text: cream on dark surfaces, leaf black on light surfaces, toned green-grey for muted labels.

## Suggested Preview and Listing Language

Neon Orchard is a bright Apocapalette theme pack for creator tools, ecommerce launches, music products, and playful digital campaigns. It pairs cyber fruit accents with grounded leaf-black and cream surfaces so the color feels loud, useful, and ready to ship.

## Avoid

- Do not make the whole theme fluorescent; leave enough dark and cream space.
- Avoid generic rainbow startup colors.
- Avoid unreadable neon text on white or bright backgrounds.
- Avoid muddy olive tones that kill the electric orchard idea.
- Avoid making success, warning, and brand all look like the same lime token.
